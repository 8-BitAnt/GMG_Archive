 1. CONTROLS:


 - Move and jump with the arrowkeys.
 - Press the downkey while standing by the gravestone to save the game. Saving may take some time.
 - Press Enter to see the Map.
 - Pressing Esc quits the game.
 - F9 takes a screenshot.
 - F4 switches fullscreen on/off.

If you get stuck to a wall, press Space to jump back to the gravestone.
