Granny and Snowman
English version 1.2

Genre: Isometric Action
Developer: Red System Team
Web: www.GMakers.ru


Anyone who likes funny games will like this shooter. You have 6 kinds of weapons, 4 special bonuses, 10 useful items and hundreds of evil snowmen who are ready to turn into shapeless piles of snow. Nine levels of unforgettable action await you!

This story happened in deep forest, where old Granny lived far away from civilization. One day a flying saucer crashed near her house, and Granny’s peaceful life came to an end...


Installation: unpack and launch Granny.exe


Verion 1.2 additions:
