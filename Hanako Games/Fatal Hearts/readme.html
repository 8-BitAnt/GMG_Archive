Introduction

In Fatal Hearts you play the role of Christina Robinson and help her to uncover the secrets behind her dreams. As the story unfolds you will be forced to make choices of who to believe, who to trust, and who to declare an enemy. Solve puzzles and conquer challenges, but be careful, or Christina may become another victim...

This game is full of decisions that will guide you to one of fourteen endings. When you reach an ending, that doesn't mean that the game is over - there's still a lot more to see! Back up and make different choices. You will meet new friends and new puzzles to solve, and things may turn out very differently.

Will Christina live or die? Will she find true love or betrayal? It all depends on you.

Controls

In Story Mode

To move ahead in the story:
    left-click the mouse OR
    press space             OR
    press enter
To hide or show the text window
(to see the picture better)
    right-click the mouse
 To reach the options menu
    left-click the mouse on the circle-cross symbol OR
    press escape
To select a choice:
    hover and left-click the mouse OR
    use up/down arrows and press enter
 To read previously displayed text:
    use the mouse scroll wheel OR
    press PageUp/PageDown
 To toggle options:
    M - music on/off
    F - windows/fullscreen
    A - change Auto Play mode
    S - change Skip Read mode

In Puzzle Mode

Each puzzle is a separate minigame with different controls. Most will use the mouse. Some will use the keyboard.

If you're not sure what to do, move the mouse to the bottom left corner of the screen and left-click on the circle-cross symbol when it appears. Select 'Display Hint'.

The first hints for each puzzle will explain the controls. If you have 'Automatic Hints' turned on in the option menu, the first hint will pop up automatically when you reach a puzzle. This should tell you what to do.

If you are still confused, select 'Display Hint' again, and more detailed instructions as well as clues to help you solve the puzzle will appear. If that still doesn't help, visit the game forums for advice!

Your game is automatically saved before you enter a puzzle. You cannot save during a puzzle. Hitting Escape during a puzzle will not take you to the options screen, but will allow you to quit the game.

Gallery

After reaching an ending, you will be able to view CGs that you have unlocked during the game in the gallery. The gallery consists of several pages and shows thumbnails of the available graphics. Left-click the mouse on a graphic to display it. The display will automatically change back to the gallery after a while, but you can speed this up by left-clicking the mouse again.

When you mouse over an unlocked graphic, you will be able to see the name of the artist.

When you mouse over any graphic, even one that is still locked, you will be able to see what chapter and branch of the story that graphic appears in. This will help you figure out where to look for graphics that you haven't unlocked yet.
