DUNGEONS & SPIDERS [v1.1b]
A Game by Leonardo Cavaletti
Original Concept by Francesco Berardi

Your goal is to escape the laybirinth with the highest score possibile without running out of lives. Note that you can't step twice on the same tile!

Move with Arrow Keys. 
Enter To Confirm.
If you remain stuck, press R to restart.
Press Z to Undo your last move.
